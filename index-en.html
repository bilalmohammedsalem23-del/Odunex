Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions from our clients

What is Odunex?
Odunex is an integrated ERP accounting system that brings all your business operations together — from accounting and inventory to HR and sales — in one easy-to-use, secure cloud platform.
Can I try the system before subscribing?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with full features, no credit card required. You can start immediately and explore all functionalities.
Does the system fully support Arabic?
Absolutely! The system is fully designed to support Arabic with RTL interface, in addition to English support. All reports and invoices can be issued in either language.
How is my data protected?
We use 256-bit SSL encryption and automatic daily backups with protected servers in certified data centers. Your data is protected by the highest global security standards.
Can the system integrate with other platforms?
Yes, Odunex supports integration with payment gateways, POS systems, e-commerce platforms, and shipping services through flexible APIs.
What technical support options are available?
We provide 24/7 technical support via phone, email, and live chat. We also offer free training upon subscription and a comprehensive library of tutorials.
Can the system be customized to my needs?
Yes, the system is flexible and fully customizable. You can add custom fields, modify workflows, and create custom reports to suit your business needs.
